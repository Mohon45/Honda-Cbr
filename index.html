The 2021 Honda CBR150R has been launched in Indonesia. The updated model comes with a much more aggressive bodywork similar to the Honda CBR250RR. It also gets a more premium Showa SFF-BP inverted front fork and a rear monoshock. Interestingly, apart from providing a more sophisticated damping, the front fork isnâ€™t preload-adjustable like the telescopic fork in its predecessor. That said, the engine is still the same 149.16cc DOHC single-cylinder liquid-cooled engine as before. It also churns out the same output: 17.1PS at 9000rpm and 14.4Nm at 7000rpm.
